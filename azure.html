Azure Standard

Your order, on your phone.

A simple personal app that wraps the real azurestandard.com store in a native shell, so you can browse, log in, fill your cart, and check out from your phone — with a branded splash and optional push reminders. Just a convenience wrapper around the site I already use.

Personal use · iOS & Android
  • Native shell
  • Real store
  • Push reminders
  • No ads, no tracking
◖ What it is

A thin shell around the real store.

01

Branded splash

The app opens to a quick splash, then loads the live mobile site.

02

The real azurestandard.com

Login, cart, and checkout all run against the actual store in a WebView — nothing is faked or proxied.

03

Push reminders

Optional push notifications (e.g., a drop-date nudge), if you allow them.

04

No ads, no tracking

Ad slots are wired but off, and the wrapper adds no analytics of its own.
