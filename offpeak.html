OffPeak

Run your home when power is cheap.

OffPeak watches real-time and day-ahead electricity prices across the U.S. markets it supports and tells you, plainly, when power is cheap and when it’s about to spike, so you can run the dryer, charge the car, or hold off, and shave real money off a variable-rate bill.

Spend less when you can
act on the price.

Wholesale power can swing from a couple of cents to over 30¢ a kWh in a day. OffPeak reads your market’s live and day-ahead prices, finds the cheap windows, and warns you before a spike — so flexible loads run when energy is at its cheapest.

Real-time-rate customersthe live market price is literally what you pay
EV ownerscharge in the dips, not the evening peak
Home-battery ownersstore cheap, discharge on spikes
The energy-curioussize up a real-time rate before switching

On a fixed-rate plan today? OffPeak still shows how the grid is pricing power right now — a useful signal, and a way to judge whether a real-time rate is worth it.
