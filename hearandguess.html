Hear and Guess

Name that tune.

Put any Spotify playlist on the big screen and let the room race to name the track. Players buzz in on their phones, server-side timing keeps it fair, and the fastest correct answer takes the round.

◖ How it works

Big screen. Everyone’s phones.

01

Host a playlist

Connect Spotify, pick any playlist, and cast the quiz to the big screen.

02

Players buzz in

Everyone joins with a QR code or a 5-letter room code and races to name the song on their phone.

03

Fastest wins

Server-side timing scores speed, wrong guesses can cost points, and playback auto-pauses on every buzz.

Anyone can play — no account needed. Hosting needs a Spotify Premium account we approve, since Spotify caps new apps until it grants full access. Ask us to be added as a host.
