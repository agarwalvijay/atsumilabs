OffPeak

Run your home when power is cheap.

OffPeak watches real-time and day-ahead electricity prices across the U.S. markets it supports and tells you, plainly, when power is cheap and when it’s about to spike, so you can run the dryer, charge the car, or hold off, and shave real money off a variable-rate bill.

◖ Smart timing

Spend less by running
your home off-peak.

Wholesale electricity can swing from a couple of cents to over 30¢ a kWh in the same day. OffPeak reads your market’s live and day-ahead prices, finds the cheap windows, and warns you before a spike — so the dishwasher, EV, and laundry run when energy is at its cheapest.

“Cheap right now — good time to run the dryer.”

Small, automatic timing decisions that quietly add up on a variable-rate plan.
