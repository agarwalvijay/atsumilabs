Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This app is unofficial and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Azure Standard. It is a personal convenience app that opens the real azurestandard.com store inside a native shell (a WebView). This policy covers the wrapper app itself — your actual shopping is governed by Azure Standard’s own privacy policy.

The important part: your shopping data isn’t ours

When you browse, sign in, fill a cart, or check out, you are using azurestandard.com directly inside the app’s WebView. Your account, order, address, and payment information are handled by Azure Standard under their systems and privacy policy. The wrapper does not intercept, read, store, or transmit your store credentials, cart, orders, or payment details.

What the wrapper itself handles

Push notifications (optional). If you allow notifications, the app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging and a device push token so it can send you reminders (for example, a drop-date nudge). The token identifies your device install, not you personally; if you deny or disable notifications, it isn’t used.

Ads. An ad SDK (Google AdMob) is included in the build but is turned off — no ads are shown and no ad-targeting data is collected. If that ever changes, this policy and the app’s store data-safety disclosures will be updated first.

No wrapper analytics. The wrapper adds no analytics or tracking of its own on top of the website.

What we do not collect

  • No Azure Standard account credentials, cart, orders, or payment data.
  • No selling of any data.
  • No advertising profiles.

Children

This is a general-audience personal app and is not directed at children under 13.

Your choices

You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings. Removing the app removes its local data; your Azure Standard account is managed on azurestandard.com.

Changes

We may update this policy; when we do, we’ll change the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this app? Email contact@atsumilabs.com. For anything about your orders or account, contact Azure Standard directly.
